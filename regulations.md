- <a href="https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/publication/2023/may/model-risk-management-principles-for-banks-ss">   SS1/23</a>. UK Bank of England. This is seen as the flagship model risk management regulation. These prescriptions initially applied to firms that used models to calculate their capital requirements, but have since been extended to all models that are materially important in banks with capital requirements. BoE suggests that these practices are adopted pervasively but are not mandating them for everyone yet. 
- <a href="https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/publication/2023/may/model-risk-management-principles-for-banks"> PS6/23 </a> wider bank of England policy towards model risk management, this covers all banks and building societies in the UK.
